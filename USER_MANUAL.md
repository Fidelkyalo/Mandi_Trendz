# 📖 Mandi Trendz User Manual

Welcome to the Mandi Trendz User Manual. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for both customers (Clients) and store managers (Admins).

---

## 🛍️ 1. Client User Manual (Customer Guide)

Experience the best of Nairobi's fashion from the comfort of your home.

### 🏠 Navigation
- **Home**: View our flagship "Lustrous Noir" hero section and featured highlights.
- **About Us**: Learn about our mission, quality standards, and the story behind Mandi Trendz.
- **Products**: Browse our full collection of handbags, totes, and accessories.
- **Testimonials**: Read what other customers say or leave your own feedback.
- **Contact Us**: Find our physical location, call us, or send an email directly.

### 🛒 Shopping & Cart
1. **Browse**: Go to the **Products** section. You can filter by category (e.g., Handbags, Tote Bags) using the filter buttons.
2. **Add to Cart**: Click the **Cart Icon** on any product to add it to your shopping bag.
3. **View Cart**: Click the floating **Cart Icon** (top right) to see your selected items.
4. **Manage Quantities**: Use the **+** or **-** buttons in the cart popup to adjust quantities.
5. **Checkout**: Click **Checkout**. This will automatically open **WhatsApp** with a pre-filled message containing your order details to finalize the purchase with us.

### ⭐ Leaving Feedback
1. Navigate to the **Testimonials** page.
2. Select a **Star Rating** (1 to 5 stars).
3. Enter your **Name** and your **Comment**.
4. Click **Post Comment**. Your feedback is saved instantly and visible to everyone!

## 🔐 2. Admin User Manual (Manager Guide)

Manage your store's inventory and keep your products up to date.

### 🚪 Accessing the Dashboard
1. Scroll to the bottom of the website and click **Admin Access** (or go to `login.html`).
2. Enter your credentials to log in to the **Admin Dashboard**.

### 📦 Inventory Management
Your dashboard allows you to control exactly what appears on the store:

#### **Adding a New Product**
1. Click the **Add Product** button.
2. Fill in the **Name** and **Price**.
3. **Categories**: Select one or more categories by checking the boxes. This allows the product to appear in multiple sections of your store (e.g., both "Tote Bags" and "Travel" categories).
4. **Image**: Either upload an image file from your computer or paste an external Image URL.
5. Add a brief **Description**.
6. Click **Save Product**.

#### **Editing or Deleting**
- **Edit**: Click the **Edit** icon on any product in the list to update its details or price.
- **Stock Status**: Click the **In Stock / Out of Stock** toggle to hide or show a product without deleting it.
- **Delete**: Click the **Trash Icon** to permanently remove a product from the shop.

### ☁️ Supabase Setup (Technical Reminder)
- **Database**: Ensure you have `products` and `testimonials` tables created.
- **Storage**: For image uploads to work, create a **Public Bucket** named `product-images` in your Supabase storage dashboard.
